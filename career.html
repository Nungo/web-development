Legendary Career

1978–1986

Humble Beginnings to a Model Discovery

Naomi Campbell first appeared in Bob Marley's *"Is This Love"* video at age 8. At 15, she was discovered while shopping in London, leading to her first British Elle cover at just 16.

1987–1997

Breaking Barriers and Supermodel Fame

Naomi became the first Black model on French Vogue's cover in 1988. She dominated the supermodel era, gracing elite catwalks and iconic covers, solidifying her status among the "Big Six."

1998–2012

Fashion Ventures and Enduring Legacy

From launching signature fragrances to walking Dior’s 60th-anniversary show, Naomi reinvented herself as a versatile icon, starring in music videos and global campaigns.

2013–2020

TV Stardom and Advocacy

Naomi embraced television with *The Face* and appeared in top campaigns. She received the CFDA Fashion Icon award and launched a YouTube series during the pandemic.

2021–Present

Global Ambassador and Creative Trailblazer

Naomi continues to lead with innovation, from her Kenyan tourism ambassadorship to a solo fashion exhibit in London, celebrating her decades of impact in fashion and beyond.
